Timeline

Quick view into long history.

  • 140-150 million years ago

    The ichthyosaurus lived

    Ichthyosaurus moved through the water. As it was carnivorous, it fed mostly on fish and smaller marine animals.

  • 2003

    Skeleton was found

    The specimen was found by Polish scientist Marcin Krystek from the Geological Museum of University Lodz

  • 2016

    Scientific article about the discovery

    Scientific article by Daniel Tyborowski was published in Polish scientific journal Acta Palaeontologica Polonica. Article link:

    Our team starts project!
